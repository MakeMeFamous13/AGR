what we do

There are 570 000 000 farms in the world, 500 000 000 of them are smaller than 2 hectares, and they cannot afford high tech devices to make their farms effective, as a result their soils are exhausting and small farmers have to put more and more chemicals for plants to grow.

We combine technologies of remote sensing and soil analyses to bring high efficiency to SMB landfarmers. We designed unique multispectral camera to reach results not available before.

Small landfarmers in major part of the world are low afficient, they do not have proper tools to run their business effectively and they do not have extra capital to invest in tech solutions. There are 500 000 000 family farms in the world by FAO data with less than 2 hectares of land.

Landfarming business often need financing to run. But there is no way to evaluate riscs and predict profit for small farms, so financing is risky. Our system alows to calculate risks and to reduce them making small farmers good clients for crediting for banks and suppliers.
